Panic Button & SOS Email Generator

In a real emergency, press the Panic Button. AIDU will prepare an email message with your details and what happened. You can send it quickly to a trusted family member, teacher or local official.

This does not automatically contact police/ambulance. You must send / call yourself.

After you click, your email app will open with a ready-made SOS message. Check quickly and press Send. At the same time, try to call ambulance / police / fire services by phone.

Tip for judges: This shows how AI can speed up human response in disasters by removing typing delays.
